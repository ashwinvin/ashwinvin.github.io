About me

I'm Ashwin Vinod and I like to make, break and fix things with computers. I'm currently experimenting with low-level programming and electronics. Oh, did I forgot to mention that I can do sick moves with nunchakus :)

About this website

The website is my contribution to the Indie Web Movement which I believe will be one of the last attempts in bringing back worthwhile organic content on the internet to read and think upon on.

The design is inspired from the cyberpunk aesthetics. The websites hosted on nekoweb had also influenced me a lot, a number of wonderful and pretty websites are hosted there, you should definitely check them out!.

I have tried my best to keep the use of LLMs here to the absolute minimum as I believe personal projects should be a reflection of ones own thoughts. As of now, the use of such here is zero :)
